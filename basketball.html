Description
Our story with the basketball team in HHSS begins back when HHSS was located in indonesia. Back then we traversed through 30km of mud everyday transporting books and toilet papers to the top of the highest mountains in the country. One day one of the men transporting the items got bored and found a funny orange ball with black lines crossing through and he was bouncing it around and he invented moves dunks and even 360 no scopes on other peoples heads. And thus backetball was born. Now HHSS has a legendary basketball teams that still stands today.
