Volleyball

Huron Heights Secondary School Teams
Description
Our volleyball story begins back when HHSS was still a top 3 global university where a bunch of students found a funny looking ball and they played soccer with it. However the ball was much lighter than the average soccer ball so they started hitting it to each other with their hands. So one day they wanted to make a net so they walked 526km to the deepest mines in Peru and collected materials to create the vollayball net. They used it to hit the ball over it and created a bunch of rules for their new sport. It eventually caught traction by the egyptian supreme court and here is where the team stands today.
